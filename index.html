THE SCIENCE

Why suction cups work on windows.
And nowhere else.

Suction cups require atmospheric pressure differential to function. Rough surface? Pressure equalizes. Dusty surface? Seal breaks. Hot car? Rubber softens, seal fails. This isn't a product flaw — it's physics.

Van der Waals adhesion is different in kind, not just degree. It operates at the molecular level: 504,000 mushroom-tip PDMS microfibers each make intimate molecular contact with a surface, creating attractive forces that don't depend on smooth geometry, sealed air chambers, or perfect conditions. The same forces hold a gecko to a ceiling at 35°C in 90% humidity. They hold your phone to a dashboard on the 405 at 2pm in August.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

How is this different from Octobuddy?
Octobuddy uses 24 silicone suction cups with permanent peel-and-stick adhesive backing. It works on smooth surfaces — mirrors, glass, clean tile. That's roughly 3 of the 8 common surfaces you encounter. Gecko-Grip Pro uses 504,000 PDMS biomimetic microfibers operating via Van der Waals forces — the same mechanism real gecko feet use. It works on 8 surface types including dashboards, textured walls, wood, and concrete. It's MagSafe-detachable (not permanent), self-cleaning, and rated for 80,000+ cycles. Same concept, fundamentally different technology, $10 more.
Is this actually gecko technology?
Yes. Synthetic PDMS microfibers replicating gecko foot spatulae. Van der Waals adhesion mechanism published in PNAS 2000 by Autumn & Full at UC Berkeley. The same class of technology has since been adopted in semiconductor handling (Samsung, TSMC, Intel), aerospace (NASA), and automotive (Ford). We scale it for consumer phone mounting.
How strong is the hold? Will my phone fall?
22 lbs on glass. Your iPhone 16 Pro Max weighs 0.50 lbs. That's a 44× safety factor. On your car dashboard (16 lbs), you have a 32× margin. Even on concrete — the hardest surface — 6 lbs gives you a 12× margin. Note: these are engineering projections based on fiber density calculations. Physical prototype validation is underway before final marketing claims are locked.
Does it leave residue? My landlord already hates me.
Zero. Van der Waals is a physical intermolecular force, not a chemical bond. Nothing is transferred to the surface. Peel it off, the wall looks exactly as it did before. This is why it also self-cleans — rinse under running water and the fibers reset. No adhesive to reapply.
Do I need the PowerClip case for Gecko-Grip?
No. Gecko-Grip Pro is MagSafe-compatible and works with any MagSafe device or case. It's a standalone product at $29 early bird. That said, the $149 Ecosystem Bundle pairs it with the PowerClip Pro case for a 32% savings and gives you the full 7-feature experience.
How long does the gecko pad last?
80,000+ attachment cycles. At 20 mounts per day, that's 10+ years of daily use. When adhesion feels reduced, rinse under running water for 30 seconds. Full grip restored. No replacement pads. No consumables.
When does it ship?
Manufactured by New Ideas Manufacturing in Denver, CO. Early bird backers: Q2 2026. Standard tier: Q3 2026. We build in buffer for hardware. These are targets, not guarantees — hardware campaigns have real timelines and we won't ship until it's right.
What's the warranty?
1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. The gecko pad has an expected service life of 10+ years under normal use. If adhesion decreases before that, rinse it first. If that doesn't fix it, we will.
